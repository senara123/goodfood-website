Egg Cheese Pizza

Making an egg cheese pizza is a delightful and simple process. Here's a basic recipe to create one at home:

prep time

25 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended for your pizza dough (usually around 450째F - 500째F or 230째C - 260째C).

Step 2

Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone, baking sheet, or pizza pan lined with parchment paper.

Step 3

Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

Step 4

Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce-covered dough.

Step 5

Carefully crack the eggs onto the pizza, dispersing them evenly over the cheese and sauce. You can crack the eggs directly onto the pizza or crack them into a bowl first and then pour them onto the pizza.

Step 6

Season the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.

Step 7

If desired, tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the pizza.

Step 8

Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, the cheese is bubbly, and the egg whites are set but the yolks are still slightly runny. Keep an eye on the pizza to prevent overcooking the eggs.

Step 9

Once done, carefully remove the pizza from the oven. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top for extra flavor.

Step 10

Allow the pizza to cool for a couple of minutes before slicing it into wedges. Serve hot and enjoy your egg cheese pizza!

ingredients

Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

1 cup pizza sauce or marinara sauce

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

4-6 eggs (depending on the size of the pizza)

Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Salt and pepper to taste

Olive oil for drizzling
