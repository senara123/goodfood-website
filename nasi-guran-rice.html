Nasi Guran Rice

"Nasi Goreng" is an Indonesian fried rice dish that's flavorful and relatively simple to make. Here's a basic recipe for Nasi Goreng:

prep time

15 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

Step 1

Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic, chopped onion, and red chilies. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

Step 2

Add the cooked protein of your choice and mixed vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

Push the ingredients to one side of the skillet and add a little more oil if needed. Crack the eggs into the skillet and scramble them until cooked through. Mix the scrambled eggs with the rest of the ingredients.

Step 3

Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir-fry the rice together with the other ingredients.

Step 4

Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir-fry the rice together with the other ingredients.

Step 5

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue cooking for a few more minutes until everything is heated through.

Step 6

Serve the Nasi Goreng hot, garnished with fried shallots or green onions, sliced cucumber, tomato, and lime wedges on the side.

ingredients

3 cups cooked and cooled rice (preferably day-old rice)

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 small onion, finely chopped

1-2 red chilies (adjust according to spice preference), finely chopped

1-2 cups of your choice of protein (chicken, shrimp, tofu, etc.), cooked and diced

1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers), chopped

2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 tablespoon fish sauce (optional)

Salt and pepper to taste

2 eggs (optional), fried or scrambled

Sliced cucumber and tomato for garnish

Fried shallots or green onions for garnish

Lime wedges for serving
